FAQs

What is an E-book ? +

An eBook is an electronic version of a traditional print book that can be read by using a personal computer or by using an eBook reader.

Do you need Internet for eBooks? +

Once you download ebooks and audiobooks, you can read or listen to them without an internet connection

Can you print an eBook? +

It is not possible to print a whole eBook. This is because of restrictions imposed by the publishers and by copyright. However, you can usually print a percentage of each eBook. You can also download some of our eBooks to your own computer or device.

Which is better eBook or textbook? +

Considering that more time is spent reading online texts, it is no surprise that readers learn more and at a faster rate while reading printed textbooks. Students are better off reading hard copy textbooks than e-books because they absorb information more effectively. Reading online can also cause health problems.
